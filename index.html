Artificial intelligence

I employ artificial intelligence to solve challenging engineering problems by learning hidden governing rules. For metal 3D printing, my collaborators and I created a tool to inform process control in achieving the desired mechanical properties in the 3D printed part. We achieved a 5000X speed up in our prediction speed using an artificial intelligence model trained on computational simulations. I am working on similar approaches for problems in mechanics and asset management.
